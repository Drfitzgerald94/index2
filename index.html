What is facilities management?

Facilities management is the professional coordination of a building's essential systems—mechanical, electrical, and plumbing—ensuring they operate safely, efficiently, and in compliance with local regulations.

For property owners and managers, effective FM means uninterrupted operations, protected asset values, and the confidence that critical systems are in expert hands.
